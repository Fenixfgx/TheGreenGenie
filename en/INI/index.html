Our History

The Green Genie company began its journey in the flower and foliage industry after years of searching for job opportunities. After gaining experience in the domestic trade, the decision was made to expand internationally and export high-quality products such as ruscus, hypericum, cocculus, baby blue eucalyptus, among others. We are located in Cachipay, Cundinamarca, Colombia, and we have a dedicated team that handles the entire production process, from planting and cultivating to carefully cutting and selecting stems for export.
