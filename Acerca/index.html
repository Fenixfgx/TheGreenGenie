About Us

Our History

The company The Green Genie began its journey in the flower and foliage industry after years of searching for job opportunities. After gaining experience in the domestic trade, the decision was made to expand internationally and export high-quality products such as ruscus, hypericum, cocculus, baby blue eucalyptus, among others. We are located in Cachipay Cundinamarca, Colombia, and have a dedicated team that oversees the entire production process, from planting and cultivating to carefully cutting and selecting stems for export.

Mission

To be a family company that produces and exports foliage with high-quality standards, through technified and environmentally sustainable cultivation, resulting in innovative products to meet and satisfy market demands.

Vision

The Green Genie aims to establish and maintain itself as a leader in the international market by offering new products, achieving recognition, credibility, and reliability through excellent service.
